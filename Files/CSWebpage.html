Join as a student

If you want to earn a degree in computer science and build an exciting career, you've come to the right place.  The Computer Science Department at Tufts offers all of its students the opportunity and flexibility to participate in groundbreaking research and collaborate with faculty and peers in many different areas. Our interdisciplinary approach to study sets us apart from traditional, highly structured computer science departments. As such, our students gain a strong foundation in computer science while preparing themselves to use their degree to solve real-world challenges.
